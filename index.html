Top three activities to do in Estonia

Old castle

Rakvere draws in visitors with its impressive 13th century castle ruins, contemporary town square and spa culture.

Bogs

Estonian bogs have gone through a large-scale transformation from being seen as a horrific place to becoming one of the most loved landscapes.

Sandy caves

Piusa Caves are an interesting intersection between the natural and human worlds, with an interesting connection to Estonian glass-making.
